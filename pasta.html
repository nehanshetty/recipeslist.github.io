Cream Cheese Pasta

Delicious cozy cream cheese pasta ready in 10 minutes, anyone? This super easy and cheap pasta recipe makes the perfect midweek last-minute meal.The cream cheese sauce is embarrassingly easy to make, but it turns your plain pasta dish into something good.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Egg breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Cook your pasta until al dente, Saute garlic in olive oil until fragrant.

step 2

Add cream cheese, pasta cooking water, parmesan, and stir well, drain and add pasta to the skillet.

step 3

Toss until well combined, adding some pasta water if needed. Serve with extra parmesan cheese, black pepper and a good drizzle of olive oil and enjoy!

ingredients

Whole grain or regular pasta, Cream cheesem, Parmesan cheese

Olive oil, Garlic

Chili flakes (optional), Salt & pepper

Nutritional Information
